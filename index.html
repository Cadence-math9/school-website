FAQ's

Here are some FAQ's people from BRS ask:

When will I get my refund? Answer: All refunds will be processed at the end of the school year. Refunds can take up to 12 weeks to process.

How much will I get back? Answer: The amount you will get back will depend on the amount of fees that were paid and the number of credits you have on your account.

My child has been required to isolate, will I be refunded for the days they needed to stay home? Answer: No. Refunds are only offered for days the CBE is not able to provide transportation or noon supervision services due to provincial direction to move to at-home learning.
